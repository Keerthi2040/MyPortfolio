Research Overview

  • Conducted semi-structured expert interviews to analyze the use of data analysis tools in Google Cloud Platform (GCP), focusing on real-world applications and perceptions.
  • Engaged five technical participants from an academic setting to gather diverse insights, and analyzed their responses using Braun & Clarkeâ€™s thematic analysis method.
  • Identified BigQuery, Dataflow, and Dataprep as the most frequently discussed tools, with BigQuery leading in popularity due to its scalability and built-in security.
  • Derived core themes such as data transformation pipelines, secure storage, and real-time analytics workflows, highlighting strengths and limitations of each tool.
  • The research offers a user-centered perspective on tool adoption in GCP, aiming to bridge the gap between academic understanding and practical implementation.
